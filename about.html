About This Blog

This blog is a coursework submission for module 6E7V0013 Digital Manufacturing at Manchester Metropolitan University. It documents my learning journey throughout the semester as I take on the role of a plastic casing manufacturer supplying to the fictional company Phones-R-Us.

The module challenges students to apply additive manufacturing (3D printing), digital scanning and metrology, process capability analysis, and plant simulation (Tecnomatix) to plan and deliver a realistic manufacturing scenario: producing both top and bottom phone casings to a standard that allows seamless integration with a master assembly and a production rate of 50,000 units per annum.

Each weekly post reflects on the practical sessions, the decisions made, and the lessons learnt — combining hands-on lab work with peer-reviewed literature and Industry 4.0 theory.
